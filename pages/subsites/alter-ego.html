ALTER EGO

A sprite-based artificial intelligence program with plug-and-play personality implementation.

What is ALTER EGO?

ALTER EGO is an AI companion platform available as both a Progressive Web App and a native desktop application. It brings AI companions to life through animated sprites and customizable personalities, combining OpenAI language models with ElevenLabs voice synthesis for interactive, lifelike characters. The native app goes further — running fully contained and portable on your local machine, no cloud dependency beyond your API keys.

Features

Sprite Characters

Animated sprite-based characters with expressive visual states and reactions.

Plug-and-Play Personalities

Swap personality modules to completely change how your AI companion behaves and responds.

Voice Synthesis

ElevenLabs-powered voice output for natural, spoken conversations.

PWA

Install as a native app on any device — works offline with local data storage via Dexie.js.

Native App Desktop Exclusive

The native desktop build runs fully contained and portable on your local machine. Your API keys never leave your device — the app communicates directly with AI providers without any intermediary server.

Desktop Overlay

Renders your companion as a persistent overlay on your desktop, always present without occupying a browser tab.

LAN Support

Expose ALTER EGO over your local network so two instances of the app can communicate and share data seamlessly.

Fully Portable

Self-contained — runs from any directory with no installation required. Take it anywhere.

Local & Private

All processing stays on your machine. Only LLM and voice API calls leave your device, using your own keys.
