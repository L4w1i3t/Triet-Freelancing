# Discord Community Starter Pack

A compact setup reference for small servers.

## Channel Map

- #welcome
- #announcements
- #rules
- #introductions
- #general
- #questions
- #showcase
- #resources
- #support
- #mod-log

## Starter Roles

- Owner
- Admin
- Moderator
- Helper
- Member
- New Member
- Muted
- Bot

## Rule Snippets

- Be clear and respectful.
- Keep conversations in the correct channels.
- Do not spam, raid, or intentionally disrupt the server.
- Do not share private information.
- Follow platform terms and local laws.
- Moderators may remove content that creates avoidable risk or confusion.

## Automation Ideas

- Welcome message with next steps.
- Auto-role after rules acknowledgement.
- Slow mode in high-traffic channels.
- Mod log for deletes, bans, and timeout actions.
- Simple support ticket flow for private help.

## Maintenance Rhythm

- Review channels monthly.
- Archive unused channels instead of leaving clutter.
- Revisit roles when the community grows.
- Keep rules short enough that people will actually read them.
